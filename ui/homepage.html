My First Blog:'Destiny'

They say it is predetermined,written in the stars a billion miles away.Some say it is all in our hands:'Hath ki lakire',while for others it is 'Bhagwan Bharose'.So what is it?That is the question which has intrigued me ,then and now and also the entire mankind as long as it exists.Whether it exists or not,what is it that thing which makes one person a king and the other just a citizen ? What is it which has limited us till now to carve our own 'Destiny'?

Let us think a little more deeply.See your life as someone's else life and think that whatever this person which is 'you' has achieved,received or experienced was his/her destiny.Well,think again!Will you really call it your destiny?Whatever we are today,we are a product of our experiences,our thoughts,our actions and circumstances which we live in.It's not about the chances we get but it's about the choices we make.Our choices determine us.

-Shaurya
